# How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please send an email request to dev@starry.sg from the email address associated with your account. This helps us verify that you are the account owner. Our team will process your request and send you a confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Example email template:

```text

Subject:
Account Deletion Request

Body:

Hello,

Please permanently delete my Slax Reader account associated with this email address: `[Your Email Address]`

I understand this action is irreversible and will remove all my saved articles and data.

Thank you, `[Your Name]`

```
