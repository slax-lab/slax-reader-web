# Slax Reader Account Deletion Notice

To ensure the security of your account, the following conditions must be met before your deletion request takes effect:

## 1. Account Security Verification Required

Please ensure you can log in to your account normally and complete the identity verification process.

## 2. Account Assets Settled and Subscription Relationships Resolved

### 2.1 Subscription Services

- **No Active Subscriptions**: If you have any active subscriptions, please cancel them first before attempting deletion
- **No Active Collection Subscriptions**: If you have subscribed to any collections, please cancel these collection subscriptions first
- **No Free Subscription History Issues**: If you have free subscription records preventing deletion, please contact customer support

### 2.2 Creator Account

- **Collections Have No Subscribers**: If you are a creator and your collections have subscribers, please handle the related collections first (transfer, delete, or wait for subscription periods to end)
- **Stripe Connect Disconnected**: If your account is linked to a Stripe Connect payment account, please disconnect it first

All assets, expected revenue, and benefits associated with your account have been settled, refunded, cleared, or voluntarily waived, and all transactions have been completed or voluntarily waived.

## 3. Account Authorization and Bindings Removed

The account has been disconnected from authorization or binding relationships with third-party products and platforms (such as Google, Apple social account logins), and there is no incomplete or disputed content in related services accessed through this account.

---

## Common Deletion Failure Reasons

If you encounter errors when deleting your account, please follow these guidelines:

| Failure Reason | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| You have active subscriptions | Go to "My Subscriptions" page, cancel all active subscriptions, and wait for the subscription period to end before retry |
| You have subscribed to some collections | Go to "My Subscriptions" page, cancel all collection subscriptions before retry |
| Your collections have subscribers | As a creator, please handle your collections first: delete collections, transfer ownership, or wait for all subscription periods to end |
| Your account is linked to Stripe Connect | Go to "Creator Settings" page, disconnect Stripe Connect before retry |
| You have free subscription records | Please contact the Slax Reader customer support team, and we will assist you |

## Important Notice

- After account deletion, all your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered, and you cannot re-register the same account within 30 days
- Please ensure you have backed up any content you wish to keep before deletion
- If you have any questions, please contact customer support
